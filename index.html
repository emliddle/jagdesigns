THE ARTIST Jim Gregory Trained in glassblowing and neon art at PRATT Fine Arts Center in Seattle, Jim Gregory has spent decades evolving a practice that moves between the precision of jewelry design and the scale of public installation. He works from two studios he built by hand in Abiquiu, New Mexico.
